## Notes on sources

Episode citations follow the standard Season×Episode format used by Wikipedia, IMDb, and the major Seinfeld fan archives. Where an item is established across the series rather than in a single episode (e.g. apartment numbers, recurring catchphrases, casting facts), that is noted in the source line.

A handful of questions reference recurring jokes that span multiple episodes (Festivus, Vandelay, Del Boca Vista, etc.). For those, the citation points to the *first* major appearance — the episode that established the gag — rather than every episode that revisits it.

If you spot an error, open an issue or PR on the repo.
